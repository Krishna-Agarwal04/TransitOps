# 🚚 TransitOps

> Smart Transport Operations Platform built for the **Odoo Hackathon 2026**.

## 📌 Overview

TransitOps is a modern transport operations platform designed to simplify fleet management by digitizing vehicle registration, driver management, trip scheduling, maintenance tracking, fuel logging, expense management, and operational analytics.

The platform helps organizations manage their transport operations efficiently while enforcing business rules and providing real-time operational insights.

---

## ✨ Key Features

- 🔐 Secure Authentication & Role-Based Access
- 🚚 Vehicle Registry Management
- 👨‍✈️ Driver Management
- 🛣️ Trip Planning & Dispatch
- 🔧 Vehicle Maintenance Tracking
- ⛽ Fuel & Expense Management
- 📊 Dashboard with KPIs
- 📈 Reports & Analytics
